About

Us
Guj Info Petro Limited

We’re a Software Company

That Provide Solutions

Viewing the current trend and spiraling rise of Internet Services,

Gujarat State Petroleum Corporation Ltd. (GSPC A Govt. of Gujarat

Undertaking), a pioneer in fulfilling the nation's energy needs, has

arisen to the technological scenario. Its conducive and requisite

infrastructure has now propelled it to enter digital vistas, which

has led to the formation of Guj Info Petro Ltd. (GIPL). In a very

short time frame Guj Info Petro Ltd. has successfully established

itself as one of the leading TSP of Gujarat.

Mission

To act proactively with the latest state of the art

emerging technology and make Gujarat a leader in

Digital India.
