ABOUT
Shri Mahesh Gohel (CEO)
Guj Info Petro Limited

Shri Mahesh Gohel, is a dynamic and versatile Information Technology

Expert with cross-functional experience and comprehensive business /

technical skill set for handling large-scale assignments (from

conceptualization to execution) on various platforms and technologies.

Shri Gohel is having more than 24 years of experience in development,

implementation of sustainable innovative IT projects.

 

Shri Mahesh Gohel is having thorough understanding of ERP

implementation with strong domain knowledge of e-Governance, Travel

and Co-operative Sector and he exhibits excellence in establishing and

implementing information solutions right from the scratch. His efforts are

recognized at national level and awarded 10 awards for e-Governance/

m-Governance and was also finalist for Prime Minister Award for

Excellence in Public Administration.

KEY MANAGERIAL PERSON
Guj Info Petro Limited

Viewing the current trend and spiraling rise of Internet Services,

Gujarat State Petroleum Corporation Ltd. (GSPC A Govt. of Gujarat

Undertaking), a pioneer in fulfilling the nation's energy needs, has

arisen to the technological scenario. Its conducive and requisite

infrastructure has now propelled it to enter digital vistas, which has

led to the formation of Guj Info Petro Ltd. (GIPL). In a very short time

frame Guj Info Petro Ltd. has successfully established itself as one

of the leading TSP of Gujarat.
